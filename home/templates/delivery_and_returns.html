Delivery and Returns

Delivery

Currently we offer a standard delivery option for free. Your shoes will be with you ready to race in no more than 5 working days.

Returns

If you are unhappy with your shoes or they don't fit then you can send them back to us within 30 days of your purchase date.

You can send them back to our returns address:

Unit 19962, Courier Point, 13 Freeland Park, Wareham Road, Poole, Dorset, BH16 6FH, UK. We can't pay for the postage and packaging and can't accept any liability if they are lost in the post.

When we send you your shoes, you'll receive a label with them with your order number on. You MUST include this with your order. If the label is missing we will return it to you.

Once we receive your shoes and it has been inspected we will refund your payment within 7 working days.

Your shoes must be in perfect condition and in their original packaging including any spikes and accessories. If they are not we will return them to you.

If your shoes are faulty we will refund your purchase within 7 working days.

Please let us know if you would like a refund or exchange by email so we can process your return as quickly as possible at shoepersonic@gmail.com.
