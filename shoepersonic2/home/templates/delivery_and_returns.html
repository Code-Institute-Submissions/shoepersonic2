Delivery and Returns

Delivery

Currently we offer a standard delivery option of first class royal mail for free. We're based in Kent so get in touch to arrange to pick them up as long as it is not too far.

Returns

If you are unhappy with your shoes or they don't fit you can send them back to us within 30 days of purchase as long as they are in perfect condition with all the labels attached.

Please let us know if you would like a refund or exchange by email so we can process your return as quickly as possible.
